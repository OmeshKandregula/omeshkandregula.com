Why RSVP

The research that shaped every product decision.

Every saccade, the eye's jump between words, is a chance for attention to drift. RSVP flashes words at one fixed point: Zero saccades.

Key Finding · Peer-Reviewed · PubMed 2023

+13% comprehension for ADHD adults, RSVP vs normal text

Neurotypical readers: No gain · Study data, not Fovea usage data

What the research changed in the product

Fixation

ORP letter in coral red at a fixed position. Eyes never move.

Ramp-up

Start at 80% of baseline WPM, ramping over 30 seconds.

Zero UI

Nothing on screen to context-switch to mid-session.

Forgiveness

Pause and rewind assume zoning out. Never punish it.
