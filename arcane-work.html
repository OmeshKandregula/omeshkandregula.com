Project: Arcane

Arcane

AI Intelligence Agent for Decision-Makers

Prodhacks 2026 Built at Prodhacks • MVP • AI Agents

Built end-to-end as part of a week-long hackathon at CMU.

Summary

An AI agent that scans the internet on your behalf and delivers a single, high-signal news update daily directly to your inbox.

Context

Founder & Product Lead MVP • 1 Week

Built end-to-end as part of a week-long hackathon at CMU named Prodhacks. Focused on user research, problem framing, product strategy, and rapid MVP execution.

The Problem

Staying informed today is cognitively expensive. Users actively struggling with information fatigue often:

  • Check multiple platforms and aggregators daily.
  • Open several articles on the same topic but abandon them halfway.
  • Feel constant anxiety about missing something critical.
  • Suffer from decision fatigue due to information abundance.

What I Built

Designed and shipped a functional MVP during the hackathon.

  • AI-driven source scanning workflow
  • Relevance filtering and cross-source synthesis logic
  • Personalized email digest delivery system

Product Approach

Agent-led, not feed-based. Designed to make decisions, not maximize engagement.

Optimized for Clarity over Clicks, and Signal over Volume.

Constraint forces quality: delivering only one update per day.

Outcomes

Shipped MVP

Built the full system independently in under a week.

Validated Demand

Confirmed problem hypothesis through user interviews.

Clear Roadmap

Defined monetization and scalable personalization strategies.

Skills: AI Agents Information Systems Product Strategy Personalization MVP Development

Research & Validation

The gap in the market

The pain isn't reading. The pain is filtering.

During research at Prodhacks, interviews revealed that professionals don't need more content. They need Signal, Context, and Impact. The majority of interviewed professionals indicated a strong preference for a single, reliable daily digest over continuous feeds.

Validation Data

78% feel overwhelmed by source volume
75% check 3 or more platforms daily
72% abandon long-form articles midway
65% feel unsure if they missed something

User Journey

The broken loop before Arcane

01

Intent

Desire to stay informed on industry trends.

02

Hunt

Jump blindly across multiple sources and aggregators.

03

Fatigue

Scroll through filler content, mentally filtering noise, leading to fatigue.

Arcane compresses that entire journey into one structured delivery.

System Architecture

High-Level Workflow

1. Input & Pipeline

  • System receives user interests via natural language prompt.
  • Agentic pipeline scans trusted sources and aggregates raw data.

2. Filtering & Synthesis

  • Prioritizes noise vs. signal based on explicit user profile.
  • AI compresses multiple angles into one coherent briefing.

3. Impact & Delivery

  • Generates reasoning on *why* this update matters to the user.
  • Scheduled daily push directly to the user's email inbox.

Key Takeaway

Constraint increases trust.

By artificially limiting output to one item during the Prodhacks build, users trusted the curation algorithm more than if it had given them five. Signal prioritization is the true product moat.
