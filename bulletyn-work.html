System Architecture

Three stages, each an internal gate

1. Ingest & Cache

Nightly cron scans 90+ feeds. Quality filters keep only high-signal articles.

2. Enrich & Personalize

Role-specific "For You" insights per subscriber. Pre-rendered, zero AI calls at send time.

3. Deliver at 7 AM

Checks every 10 minutes for local 7 AM. Pre-built briefs ship via email.
